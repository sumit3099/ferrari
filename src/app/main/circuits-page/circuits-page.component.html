F1 Circuits

Here you can find all names of the circuits that have been used since the beginning of Formula 1 in 1950.

Names that are colored red are click-able. The circuits have their own dedicated page where you can find a lot of information like: lap records, lap details, winners, average speed, city and graphical layout.

Each circuit page contains a lot of info. You can see things like when the the first race was held, the layout, who holds the fastest lap, see all podiums of each race and see an onboard lap of the circuit and lots more.

All F1 circuits are sorted in alphabetical order.
